Description

Baigan Choka is a roasted eggplant dish made with garlic, salt, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 Eggplants
  • 5 cloves of garlic + 1 clove per eggplant
  • 1.5 (Scotch Bonnet, can replace with Habanero)
  • 2 tbs of salt

Cooking

  1. Peel garlic, one clove per eggplant
  2. Split clove and cut each eggplant on either side
  3. Place half of clove on each side of eggplant

  4. Heat eggplants until soft
    (10 minutes until each side)
  5. After the eggplants are cooked and soft, we're need to cut them open and place them in a bowl. Follow the next steps to do this.
  6. Cut off the head of the eggplant
  7. Carve one side open
  8. Open it like a book
  9. Dice into chunks without slicing the bottom skin (see image below)
  10. Scoop the inside out with a spoon and into a bowl
  11. Crush the eggplant inside the bowl
  12. Cut up 5 move cloves of garlic and 1.5 Scotch Bonnet pepper
  13. Fry until the garlic turns black and the pepper turns dark (around ten minutes)
  14. Add to the extra garlic and pepper to the eggplant bowl and crush it all together
  15. Serve and enjoy!
