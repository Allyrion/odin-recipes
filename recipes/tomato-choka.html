Description

Tomato Choka is a savory Caribbean breakfast dish of roasted or boiled tomatoes, garlic, onion and hot pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs of tomato
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 1.5 hot pepper (Scotch Bonnet/Habenero)
  • 1 onions

Cooking

  1. Wash tomatoes
  2. Cut in half and place in pan
  3. Split 5 cloves of garlic and place in the pan with the tomatoes
  4. Cut up pepper and dice 1 onion
  5. Add half the onion and pepper to pan
  6. Add water to cover the base
  7. Cover pan
  8. Cook until soft
    (Roughly 30 minutes)
  9. Crush 3 cloves of garlic and the rest of the onion and pepper separately
  10. (Optional) Roast with geera (cumin)
  11. Add the rest of the garlic, onion and pepper to the pan
  12. Add salt to taste
  13. Crush it all together (or use blender) to preferred consistency
  14. Serve and enjoy!
