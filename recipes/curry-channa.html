Curry Channa

Home

Ingredients

  • 1lb small grain channa (chickpeas)
  • 6 medium cloves of garlic
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 2 medium hot pepper (Scotch Bonnet/Habanero)
  • 2 Tbs Green seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of curry
  • 1.5 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 pot spoon of oil
  • (Optional) half a teaspoon of Geera

Prep

  1. Take out the channa (chickpeas) and check for rotten ones
  2. Rinse
  3. Put double the amount of water as the channa
  4. Soak for 3 hours

Cooking

  1. Boil the Channa
  2. Scoop out the froth
  3. 20 minutes Medium heat
  4. First add oil in new pot
  5. Then fry with Geera and Methi
  6. Then add half of the onions and all of the garlic till they get light brown
  7. Add curry and green seasoning and rest of the onion
  8. Stir for a bit and then add water as it sticks to the pot (3 minutes)
  9. Add Channa, pepper and salt (1.5 teaspoons)
  10. Turn over to mix
  11. Let it cook for 8-10 minutes
  12. Serve and Enjoy
